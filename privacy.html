Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

Dur-e-sitar (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we may collect when you use our website or contact us, and how we use it.

Information we may collect

  • Contact details you provide (for example name, phone number, delivery address) when you message us on WhatsApp or use our contact form.
  • Order details such as product names, sizes, colours, and payment/delivery arrangements discussed in chat.
  • Technical data from your browser (for example basic server logs if your hosting provider collects them) — we do not run advanced tracking by default on this static site.

How we use information

We use your information to respond to you, confirm orders, arrange delivery, and provide customer support. We do not sell your personal information.

WhatsApp and third parties

When you contact us via WhatsApp or other apps, your messages are also subject to those providers’ privacy policies. Please review Meta/WhatsApp terms if you use that channel.

Retention

We keep chat and order-related messages only as long as needed for customer service, accounting, or legal requirements, then delete or archive them according to our routine practice.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct your details or to stop using your information for marketing (if we ever send promotional messages, we will honour opt-out requests). For requests, contact us on WhatsApp.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
