About us

Dur-e-sitar is owned and run by Shanza Ali Khan. We design and source beautifully tailored abayas with a focus on honest quality, modern silhouettes, and fabrics that feel as good as they look.

We are an online-only business — there is no walk-in store. Every order starts with a conversation on WhatsApp, where we help with sizes, lengths, colours, and delivery across Pakistan.

  • Wedding, Eid, and everyday styles
  • Bulk or school orders by arrangement
