About Dur-e-sitar

Dur-e-sitar is owned and run by Shanza Ali Khan. We are an online-only store — message us on WhatsApp for orders, sizes, and styling help.

We focus on beautifully customized abayas with honest quality, modern designs, and fair pricing so every piece earns a place in your wardrobe.
